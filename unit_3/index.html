Christina's small-batch, homemade ice cream

The best ice cream in Boston!

All the inventive offerings at Christina's Homemade Ice Cream are made on premises with only the best quality and freshest of ingredients. Established in 1983, Christina's is located in fashionable Inman Square and is equal distance from both Harvard and M.I.T.

Visit us

Christina's is open from 11:30 am until 10:30 pm, Monday through Sunday. Please call for holiday hours. Keep up with our latest news by following us on Facebook.

A Boston landmark

Our Story

Christina's Homemade Ice Cream is a landmark for the culinary enthusiast and has been recognized as such by The Boston Glone, Boston Magazine, The Boston Herald, The Boston Phoenix, The Weekly Dig, The Improper Bostonian, The Phantom Gourmet, Yankee Magazine, Travel and Leisure and Bon Appetit. All of our delectable and inventive offerings are made on premises with only the best quality and freshest of ingredients. On any given day, treat yourself to any of our 50 or more exciting flavors.
